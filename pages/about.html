Fun Facts About Capybaras

Top 5 Quick Facts

  1. Capybaras are the largest rodents in the world.

  2. They can hold their breath underwater for up to 5 minutes.

  3. Capybaras are highly social and live in groups.

  4. They are excellent swimmers and love water.

  5. Capybaras are herbivores and love munching on grass!

Capybara Lifestyle

Capybaras are native to South America and are often found near rivers, lakes, and swamps. They communicate with clicks, whistles, and purrs!

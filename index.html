Capybara Nursery

Welcome to Capybara Nursery! We are a small team at Capybara Haven dedicated to rescuing and caring for baby capybaras. Our mission:

  • Rehabilitate orphaned capybaras
  • Educate people about their lifestyle
  • Provide gentle reintroduction into their natural habitat

Founded in 2022. We work with love and care!
